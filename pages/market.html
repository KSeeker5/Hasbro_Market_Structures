Market Competition & Differentiation


What is the market structure?

Hasbro Market Share: 12.26%
Mattel Market Share: 34.04%
 HHI= (12.26)2 + (34.04)2
  HHI = 1,309
That means it's an oligopoly!


What separates this company from its competitors?

Hasbro makes not only toys, but also board games. This is much different from how Mattel operates because they pretty much just focus on the toys they make.
